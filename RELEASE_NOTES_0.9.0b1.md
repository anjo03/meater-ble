# MEATER BLE 0.9.0b1

First public beta of a fully local Home Assistant integration for original MEATER and MEATER Plus probes.

Highlights: direct BLE through Home Assistant adapters and ESPHome proxies; no phone, account, or cloud; live notifications; automatic discovery and reconnect; smoothed temperatures; heating rate; inferred cook states and events; local cook history; multi-probe support; diagnostics.

Cook-state and session history are inferred and may still produce false transitions. Raw tip and ambient readings are the most mature features.
